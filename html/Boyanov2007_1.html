Translation:

NA

Commentary:

Fragment of military diploma issued on behalf of an inhabitant of Thrace, likely of Thracian origin, who served before the mid-2nd century AD in the Roman Navy (the imperial fleet Classis Praetoria Misenensis at Misenum in modern Italy).
