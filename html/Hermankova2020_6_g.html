Graffiti (?) on pottery

Description: The clay vessel base fragment. The graffiti was incised when the clay was still wet into the outer side of a vessel, possibly an open bowl. The image represents X-shaped decorative motif. Dimensions (mm) × diam.:100 (Outer diameter of the base).
Text: (Possible) letters incised into the body above the base of the vessel, on the outer side at the base.
Letters: Incised into wet clay.

Date: AD 100 - AD 399
Type of inscription: NA

Findspot: Yurta-Stroyno, Stroyno, Yambol, Bulgaria. Found at Yurta-Stroyno in 2015 in the robber's trench soil in the inner area of the house that was built after AD 217.
Original location: Unknown place in Thracia
Last recorded location: Stroyno Archaeological Project, now stored at Regional Historical Museum, Yambol, Bulgaria. Inv. no. SY15_414
