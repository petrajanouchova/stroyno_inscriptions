Fish pictorial graffiti on pottery sherd

Description: The clay vessel base fragment. The pictorial graffiti is incised into the inner side of fine red-slipped ware, possibly of an open bowl. The image represents a fish fins.
Text: No letters visible.
Letters: NA

Date: AD 100 - AD 399
Type of inscription: NA

Findspot: Yurta-Stroyno, Stroyno, Yambol, Bulgaria. Found at Yurta-Stroyno in 2015 in the robber's trench soil in the inner area of the house that was built after AD 217.
Original location: Unknown place in Thracia
Last recorded location: Stroyno Archaeological Project, now stored at Regional Historical Museum, Yambol, Bulgaria. Inv. no. SY15_419
